About Us

Empowering users with data visualization and analytics.

Our Mission

At DataVizHub, we try to make data visualization intuitive, powerful, and accessible. Our platform enables users to analyze, process, and visualize data efficiently. The users might be business analysts, researchers, or developers.

Our goal is to provide a fast data analysis experience using modern web technologies and scalable backend solutions.

Our System Architecture

Our system follows a modular, scalable architecture, leveraging Django, MySQL, and Apache ECharts for efficient data management and visualization.

The architecture is designed to handle large-scale data processing, ensuring fast responses for user interaction.
